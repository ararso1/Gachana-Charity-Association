About Us

Empowering Those in Need

At Gachana Charity Association, we believe that every act of kindness, no matter how small, can create a ripple of change. Together, we strive to build a community where everyone is supported, empowered, and no one is left behind.

Gachana Charity Association, Board

Gachana Charity Association is a non-profit organization based in Dire Dawa, founded three years ago, currently providing services within Dire Dawa, with a future goal of expanding its support across Ethiopia. The association focuses on education, healthcare, skills training, women’s empowerment, and children’s welfare to promote self-reliance and improve community well-being. .
Gachana Charity Association aim to establish youth training centers and craft-learning facilities, support women facing various hardships, create sustainable job opportunities for women, and strengthen children’s welfare. Our mission includes establishing schools from kindergarten through high school, setting up a clinic under the name of Gachana Charity Association, developing our own sustainable sources of economic income, empowering individuals to build strong and independent mindsets, and expanding employment opportunities to improve lives and strengthen the community as a whole
